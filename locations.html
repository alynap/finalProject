Istanbul, Turkey

This was a trip I took during summer '22. Traveling by car, the drive took about 9 hours. Staying in the city centre, I used all 4 days to eat amazing food, learn about Turkey's culture, and to photograph as much as the city's beauty as possible. A boat tour down the Bosphorus allowed me to photograph both the European and Anatolian side of the city from the water.

Rila, Bulgaria

I've taken two hiking trips in the Rila mountains, one to the Seven Rila Lakes, and another to Musala peak, the sixth highest peak in all of mainland Europe. Although quite a difficult journey (I hadn't packed my hiking boots!), the experience was unforgettable. This trip was taken on a national holiday, so their were much more hikers than usual. The weather was perfect, and all fellow hikers were some of the nicest people I've met. Without a doubt, I would do this experience all over again if given the chance.
